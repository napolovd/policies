Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 5, 2026

MoneyDNA is built as a freemium application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

MoneyDNA is designed with your privacy in mind. All financial data you enter into the App (transactions, budgets, goals) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob — to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — to understand how users interact with the App (e.g., screen views, feature usage). Data collected includes device information, app usage statistics, and approximate location. Firebase Privacy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — to collect crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability. This includes device state, crash stack traces, and device identifiers. Firebase Privacy
  • Apple App Store — to process in-app purchases (premium subscription). Payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple; we do not collect or store your payment information.

Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to send you reminders that you configure within the App.

Log Data

In the event of an error or crash, we collect Log Data through Firebase Crashlytics. This may include your device name, operating system version, app configuration, the time and date of use, and other diagnostic statistics.

Advertising

The free version of MoneyDNA displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to serve ads relevant to you. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. Premium users do not see advertisements.

In-App Purchases

MoneyDNA offers a premium subscription through the Apple App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details such as credit card numbers. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how Apple handles your payment data.

Data Retention

Your financial data is stored only on your device. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted. Analytics and crash data collected by Firebase is retained according to Firebase's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. You are advised to review this page periodically.
